Nightscribe · nightscribe.net

Nightscribe is a community platform for authors and readers. Its focus is on "night" themed fiction, covering various genres such as horror, thriller, mystery, sci-fi and fantasy.

Users can sign up and publish their stories, or simply browse the catalog for a good read. Nightscribe's features are very similar to YouTube, in the way that users can rate, favorite, comment and share on stories published by other authors.
